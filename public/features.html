Features


Games
Enjoy your time with included games like Minesweeper and Rock Paper Scissors.

Animals
Enjoy your time with animal photos, including birds, cats, dogs and foxes.

Rank
With Ironix you can level up thanks to our rank system. This system includes features like adding a role at a specific level, a leaderboard and freezing channels/members from winning XP.

Utilities
We offer several utilities, including a command for getting how many members are in a server, making a poll and suggestions.

Moderation
Keep your server protected! With Ironix you can kick, mute or ban permanently or temporally, you can also purge messages.

Filters
Keep your server protected! With Ironix you can blacklist words and block e-mail adresses.

Logs
Keep your server tracked! With Ironix you will be warned every time a person says a blacklisted word or an e-mail address, or if a message gets deleted or edited.

Functions
In Ironix, every feature is called “function” and they can be disabled, so mantain enabled only what you want!
